zealHMM analysis notebooks

Figures, tables, and rendered notes for the nilHMM ancestry-caller methods paper — one duration-aware 3-state HMM, four callers, calibrated on simulation and validated on real TeoNAM genotypes. The manuscript lives in its own repository; this site publishes the supporting notebooks.

Thesis. Ancestry-caller parameters (rigidity r, error err, emission means) can be calibrated from cheap simcross simulations rather than scarce high-coverage truth samples, and transfer across the Skim (low-coverage WGS) and BrB (3′ RNA-seq) modalities.
